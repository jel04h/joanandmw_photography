Joan + Matt

We're weekend warrior photographers.

For something fun to do on the weekends, a reason to buy more gear, and to bond with our community, Joan and Matt started this tiny little photography business in Seattle.

For two similar people, we do have different shooting styles. Matt is politely unobstructive and captures unexpectedly beautiful candids while Joan has a laundry list and will graciously get what she wants. We're openminded when it comes to editing/styles. Send us examples of what you like!

We're the easy going, friendly, prepared, on time, lawful/good type. We'll try to make everything as painless and easy for you as possible.

In our spare time, we like running, hiking, music, museums, boardgames, and beaches.

Let's take pictures! :D
